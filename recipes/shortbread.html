Shortbread Cookies

A Holiday Favorite

This classic shortbread cookie recipe makes 2 dozen rich, buttery, melt-in-your-mouth press cookies using just 4 simple ingredients that you probably have in your pantry. They're easy to decorate and perfect for Christmas cookie plates.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 c all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Cream softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in vanilla; add flour and mix well until combined.

  3. Step 3

    4. Fill cookie press with the dough and form cookies onto two ungreased cookie sheets, spacing them about 1 1/2 inches apart. Bake for 10 - 12 minutes, or until the edges of the cookies are just starting to turn golden brown.

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove the cookie sheets from the oven, and set them on a wire cooling rack for 2 minutes. Then transfer the shortbread cookies to the rack to cool completely.
